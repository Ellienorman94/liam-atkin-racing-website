Can beginners join Liam Atkin Racing?

Absolutely. Whether you're taking your first steps into karting, transitioning from arrive-and-drive championships such as Club100, or you're an experienced owner-driver looking to take your racing to the next level, Liam Atkin Racing welcomes drivers of all abilities. Our aim is to provide a friendly, family-oriented environment where every driver receives professional coaching, technical support and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Which karting classes do you support?

Micro, Honda, Rotax Inter, Junior Rotax and Senior Rotax. We can provide awning space for other classes as well, so please get in touch to discuss your requirements.

Do I need my own kart?

Yes. Drivers will need their own kart to join Liam Atkin Racing. Although hiring a kart is possible, it is usually a more expensive option over time. If you're unsure where to start with purchasing a kart, we're happy to offer advice and help you find the right equipment for your budget and racing goals.

What's included in your driver packages?

We offer both daily and monthly driver packages, designed to suit a range of drivers and racing commitments. Each package includes different levels of support and benefits. For a full breakdown of what's included in each package, please visit our Services page.

Do you offer testing and driver coaching?

Yes. Liam Atkin Racing offers test days throughout the year, including weekdays and selected weekends when the team isn't racing. Winter testing plays a key role in preparing drivers for the upcoming season, giving them valuable track time before racing begins. Every test day includes professional driver coaching and detailed data analysis, helping drivers understand their performance and continue improving. We also provide on-track coaching, delivering live feedback to develop confidence, consistency and racecraft.

Where does Liam Atkin Racing compete?

Our race calendar varies depending on the championships and events we compete in each season. We regularly attend club meetings at tracks including Fulbeck, PFI, Whilton Mill, Kimbolton and Shenington, as well as major championships throughout the UK. To see where we'll be racing next, please visit our Upcoming Events page.

Do parents need previous motorsport experience?

Not at all. Parents don't need any previous motorsport experience to join Liam Atkin Racing. We'll always be happy to answer questions and help you understand race weekends, regulations and what to expect. For families looking for extra support, we can also arrange a dedicated mechanic at an additional cost, while you continue to benefit from the support and atmosphere of the LAR team awning.
